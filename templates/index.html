Our Services

We provide a wide range of services to meet all your mobile needs.

1. Screen Replacement

  • Replacement of broken, cracked, or unresponsive touchscreens
  • LCD or OLED display repairs for different brands
  • Fixing screen flickering, dead pixels, or ghost touches

2. Battery Replacement

  • Replacing swollen, damaged, or rapidly draining batteries
  • Use of genuine or high-quality compatible batteries
  • Solving overheating problems related to the battery

3. Water Damage Repair

  • Drying and cleaning internal components affected by liquid exposure
  • Repairing short circuits caused by water damage
  • Replacing corroded parts like the motherboard, connectors, or charging port

4. Charging Port & Speaker Issues

  • Replacing a damaged charging port
  • Fixing slow or inconsistent charging problems
  • Repairing or replacing malfunctioning speakers, microphones, and headphone jacks

5. Software Updates & Flashing

  • Flashing firmware to fix software crashes, boot loops, or stuck devices
  • Removing viruses, malware, or unwanted applications
  • Resetting devices and optimizing software performance

6. Data Recovery & Backup

  • Retrieving lost or deleted data such as contacts, photos, and documents
  • Backing up important data before factory resets or software updates
  • Recovering data from damaged or non-functional devices
